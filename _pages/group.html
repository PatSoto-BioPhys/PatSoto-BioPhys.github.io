We use computational biophysics to study protein conformational dynamics and folding so that

  • We elucidate molecular mechanisms hallmark of neurodegenerative diseases
  • Undergraduates develop their agency and skills to tackle scientifically authentic research projects at the interface between physical and life sciences.

Check our Team Canvas to see how we operate as a group.

Engaging in research with undergraduates is deeply fulfilling:

  • Catalyzes the academic and scientific growth of a young adult
  • Offers the opportunity to see the world of scientific research through the eyes of the emerging expert
  • Brings the vitality of the youth and a science workforce that renews itself
  • Refreshes perspectives on science, undergraduate college experience, and life
  • Enriches processes and growth through mutual challenge and collaboration
